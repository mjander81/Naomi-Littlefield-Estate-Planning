Your Choices, Comprehensive and Clear

I moved to the Washington, DC area from Key Largo, FL in 2001 to attend Georgetown Law School where I earned my Juris Doctorate. Prior to starting my private practice, I worked in congressional communications and policy analysis for the U.S. Government.

I live in the beautiful and historic city of Greenbelt, Maryland with my two children and our mini-goldendoodle.

During the last 4 years, my law practice has focused almost exclusively on estate planning. This area of practice appeals to me because: I feel a tremendous sense of accomplishment when I help a person prepare documents that will make the management of their estate easier and less expensive for their loved ones. As a mom, I like working part-time, and estate planning is an area of law that is amenable to flexible scheduling.
